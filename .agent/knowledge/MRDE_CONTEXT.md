MRDE studies public weather forecast-observation mismatches. v0 starts with 2-meter temperature only, likely HRRR forecasts versus ASOS/METAR observations, joined to terrain and land-cover metadata.

## Important boundary

MRDE v0 may find residual-pattern associations. It may not claim forecast improvement, operational prediction, or causal explanation.
